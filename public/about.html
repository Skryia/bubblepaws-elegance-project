Our Story

Built on patience, kindness
and a serious love of dogs.

Bubblepaws began with a simple belief: grooming should feel as good for the dog as it looks afterwards.

Calm by design
The story

A quiet little studio with a big philosophy.

We grew up surrounded by dogs — and noticed early on how often grooming felt like an assembly line. Cages, queues, raised voices. We knew there was a kinder way to do it.

So we built one. Bubblepaws is a single-dog studio: calm music, soft handling, no rush. Whether your dog bounds in or needs a few minutes to settle, we work at their pace, not ours.

What we stand for

Our values, in plain words.

Love first

Every dog who walks through our door is treated like one of our own. Treats, cuddles, and as much time as they need.

Professional craft

Breed-specific techniques, premium products and a sharp eye for finishing — grooming as a quiet craft, not a conveyor belt.

Personal approach

We remember your dog's quirks, their favourite scratch spot and the cut you loved last time. No clipboard required.

Our mission

To make grooming day the best day of the week.

We measure success by tails, not turnover. If your dog skips into the studio next time, we've done our job.
